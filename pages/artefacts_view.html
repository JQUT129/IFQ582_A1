General Info

Used By: Bunjilaka

Location: Eastern Australia

Discovered: 1800s

Description:

The lil-lil is a type of club which was made and used only in eastern Australia. It was a dangerous weapon when used in battle. The design on the lil-lil club contains meandering loops divided into three sections. It is typical of a motif used by Aboriginal people in northern New South Wales and southeastern Queensland.
